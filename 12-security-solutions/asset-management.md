## Asset Management Solutions

### What is Asset Management?
Asset Management Solutions is software that implements all asset 
management operations such as monitoring the operating status of 
assets in the corporate network, maintaining them, and removing 
them when necessary.

### Types and Components of IT Asset Management

The four main managed IT assets are:

- Software
- Hardware
- Mobile Devices
- Cloud
