# OWASP Top 10 2025

## What is OWASP Top 10?
The OWASP Top 10 is a globally recognized list of the **most critical web application security risks**.  
It helps developers and security teams prioritize defenses and improve secure coding. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

---

## OWASP Top 10:2025 List

1. **A01:2025 – Broken Access Control**  
   Users can perform actions or access data beyond their permissions. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

2. **A02:2025 – Security Misconfiguration**  
   Weak or inconsistent system settings exposing vulnerabilities. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

3. **A03:2025 – Software Supply Chain Failures**  
   Risks from third-party libraries, dependencies, and build components. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

4. **A04:2025 – Cryptographic Failures**  
   Weak, outdated, or misused cryptography exposing sensitive data. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

5. **A05:2025 – Injection**  
   Untrusted input processed directly in commands/queries (e.g., SQLi, XSS). :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

6. **A06:2025 – Insecure Design**  
   Flaws in architecture/threat modeling that introduce systemic risk. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}

7. **A07:2025 – Authentication Failures**  
   Weak login/session controls enabling unauthorized access. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}

8. **A08:2025 – Software or Data Integrity Failures**  
   Tampering, unsigned updates, or compromised dependencies. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}

9. **A09:2025 – Security Logging and Alerting Failures**  
   Missing logs/alerts hindering incident detection. :contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}

10. **A10:2025 – Mishandling of Exceptional Conditions**  
    Improper error handling and logic flaws under abnormal conditions. :contentReference[oaicite:12]{index=12}

## Why It’s Important

- Reflects **real-world data** from millions of tested applications. :contentReference[oaicite:13]{index=13}
- Helps **secure software development lifecycle (SDLC)**.  
- Used widely in **AppSec, DevOps, and security reviews**.

---

## How To Use in Blue Team / AppSec

- Map alerts/findings to OWASP categories.
- Prioritize fixes based on risk and exploitability.
- Align secure coding standards with Top 10 controls.
